CargoBot helps to solve the problems that shippers and carriers face within the industry: inefficiency, unproductiveness, and lack of transparency in all transactions. With CargoBot your job becomes efficient and productive because the communication lines between shippers and carriers become direct, all documents are electronically stored, and invoices and rate of confirmationâ€™s are generated by our system. CargoBot provides you business with transparency. There are no hidden fees, all bids on shipments are visible by all carriers, carriers decide how they get paid and know exactly how much net income they will be making for each job they perform, and all carriers insurance and operating authority are stored within our system for shippers to have access to when working with the carrier.

CargoBot is not a loadboard because with CargoBot carriers bid directly online for each job and that bidding is visible to all other carriers. Unlike loadboards, shippers negotiate directly with carriers. CargoBot simply provides the platform to allow both parties to meet but never gets involved in negotiations.

CargoBot is a free platform that helps connects shippers and carriers so that they may perform business directly with each other, without brokers. CargoBot allows you to complete all of your freight shipments in their entirety through our platform.

CargoBot pays carriers how they choose to be paid: upon POD, 7 days, 15 days, 30 days, or carriers can use their own factoring company. Fuel advances are also offered. We guarantee carriers their payment and hold no money back from carriers, we pay out 100% of their payment.

Shippers, fleet companies, and owner operators can use CargoBot.

CargoBot prescreens all carriers prior to allowing them join the network of carriers. Carriers insurance, Operating Authority, References, FMCSA record are verified by CargoBot prior to joining and while they are using CargoBot.

With CargoBot shippers have the ability to post their shipments online and multiple carriers bid on the load. Once a carrier wins a bid and has decided how they want to get paid a rate of confirmation is generated by CargoBot and must be signed by the carrier. The load hits the road and both parties have the ability to track the shipment with the GPS tool. Once it arrives to its delivery point the carrier uploads its POD through the app and an invoice is generated by CargoBot. All paperwork is stored and generated within the system. At the end of the transaction both parties have the ability to rate each other so others can use the criteria for future use.

CargoBot is free of charge for shippers. Carriers can also join free of charge adn will have the ability to receive loads and see what shippers are posting. However, once a carrier closes on a rate for a load and completes the job they will be charged a set fee per transaction (this fee is a set percentage and does not alter based on the monetary value of the transaction).

In order to make a claim, the consignee must specify in the POD why the load was rejected, if the consignee fails to do so no claim will be accepted. To start the process carrier and shipper must take pictures of the documentation and status of the cargo and send them to our customer service line at 305-749-5490 or at customerservice@cargobot.io, stating the load number and a brief explanation of what happened.
